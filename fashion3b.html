Meet Delali

To celebrate the first month of Coachtopia, we’ve collaborated with 26-year-old photographer and Coachtopian Delali Ayivi to capture the Ergo, Wavy Dinky and more in colourful settings inspired by circularity. Currently based between New York and Lomé, Togo, Delali is focused on diversifying representations of Black and Togolese communities across the globe, working with circularity in mind by reusing sets and sourcing vintage garments items for her shoots.
